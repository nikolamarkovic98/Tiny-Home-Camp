About tinyHouse Camp

Tiny is the largest tiny house camp, famous for it's high quality tiny homes where people from different parts of the world gather to share experience and hangout. Tiny was founded in 2000 and has over 1 million visits every year.

We strive to improve to create an experience that you will remember for a long time!

Beautiful Environment

Our tiny camps are located at the healthiest and breath taking locations that you only dream about.
